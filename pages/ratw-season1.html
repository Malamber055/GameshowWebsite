Race Around the World Season 1

Race Around the World: Season 1 is the first installment of the Roblox Reality TV Show Game: Race Around the World. Race Around the World

Development

Applications for Race Around the World Season 1 were released on the 24th of January 2022. Marking it to be the first ever season to be held since Outlaster 3.

The season was split into 5 different episodes. In which 2 eliminations would occur in each episode. Originally the Season was supposed to be a continuation of the Amazing Race series but was later changed to Race Around the World. Episodes aired throughout February 6th to February 14th 2022.
