Race Around the World - Season 2

Race Around the World: Season 2 is the second installment of the Roblox Reality TV Show Game: Race Around the World. Race Around the World

Development

Applications for Race Around the World Season 2 were released on the February 18th 2022, the original cast was revealed on the 24th, before the revised cast was released on the 26th. The Season itself was recorded later that day.

The first episode was uploaded on April 3rd 2022, however, due to Skelly's Hiatus the next episode was not released until December 23rd 2022.
